Dave Grohl - Rock's Coolest Dude

About Dave

Dave Grohl is not only one of the most talented multi-instrumentalists on the planet, he has also built himself a solid reputation as one of Rock 'n Roll's nicest people.

Apart from his role as the frontman of one of the worlds largest stadium filler bands ("The Foo Fighters"), Dave Grohl has been known to wear many other hats and has been involved in numerous projects as a leader, collaborator, musician, producer for many bands such as Kiss, Ghost, Rush, Led Zeppelin, Them Crooked Vultures, Nirvana, Queens of the Stone Age etc to name a few.

Notable Achievements and Awards

  • 2011 - NME Award - Godlike Genius
  • 2016 - NME Award - Hero of the Year
  • 2018, 2014 - Grammy Award - Best Rock Song

Another major honor for Dave Grohl was when his beloved hometown of Warren, Ohio unveiled a gigantic 902 lb (409 kg) set of drumsticks in 2012 to honor him. According to The Hollywood Reporter, the massive pair broke the Guinness World Record.
